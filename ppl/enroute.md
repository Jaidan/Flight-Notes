Enroute
=======

Mapping
-------

Lat and Lng grid

Equator is 0 Lat.

Lng is through the poles (meridians

Time
----

All time is done in UTC.  15 degree meridians are used for timezones.
Midnight is 0000. UTC is always expressed in 24 hours time.  UTC does not have UTC.


Charting
--------
